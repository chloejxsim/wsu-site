competitions

debating

regionals

Regionals is an annual debating tournament for Years 9 to 13. Students of any ability are able to participate in Wellington's largest debating competition on the calendar.

impromptu cup

Impromptu cup is an annual competition for Years 9 to 13. Students are only given 10 minutes to prepare their team's case, and they are in teams of two.

british parliamentary

The Wellington British Parliamentary Tournament invites debaters from Year 9 to 13 to try the British Parliamentary debating format. Students are placed in teams of two, with four teams per each debate.

moot must fall

Moot Must Fall is a recently created debating tournament pitched as a Junior Development tournament. It is done in collaboration with senior debaters around Wellington, and Wellington Speaking Union. It is open to Years 9 to 11.

public speaking

dunsheath shield

The Dunsheath Shield is a public speaking competition open to Years 7 to 9. The best public speakers from each school are invited to participate, and give a speech of 5 to 6 minutes.

stockley cup

The Stockley Cup is a public speaking competition open to Years 9 to 13. The best public speakers from each school are invited to participate, and give a speech of 7 to 8 minutes.
